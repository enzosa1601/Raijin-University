who we are?

The Raijin educational institution originated in 2020 from a literacy class located in the modest neighborhood of Duque de Cacxas, a city in Rio de Janeiro. In 2021, the institution's founder, Professor Farid Germano Filho, received the title of Educator of the Year.

The founder's dream came true with the creation of the Raijin School of Engineering, in 2020, authorized by Decree No. 70,828, of July 13, 2020. Since its origin, the sense of mission, the fundamental values ​​of education and the guidelines present in the Raijin permeated the creation of its faculties and guided its actions, giving rise to a latent desire for integration, which was consolidated with its unification in the Integrated Colleges Raijin, in 2021.

Finally, at a meeting held on November 10, 2021, the Federal Council of Education unanimously approved - an act ratified by Ordinance No. 1725, of November 20, 2020 -, the transformation of the Integrated Colleges Raijin into Raijin University (RNU). The history and scenario described here do not exhaust the institution's achievements. Rather, they represent a description of the significant milestones in its more than seven decades of existence, characterized by the spirit of collaboration in its actions and constant integration with its community.
